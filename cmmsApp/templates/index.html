Types

Comprehensive Guide to Various Heating Element Types for Industrial and Commercial Thermal Equipment

Explore All Types

Space Heaters

These small, portable devices are used inside electrical and mechanical equipment enclosures to maintain temperature and minimize moisture. They are crucial in situations where the main heating system in a building is inadequate or when condensation must be prevented.

Tubular Heaters

A staple in industrial heating, tubular heaters are versatile, used for heating liquids, solids, and gases. They utilize all three types of heat transfer: conduction, convection, and radiation. Their robust design makes them suitable for a wide range of multipurpose heating applications.

AHU Heaters (Air Handling Unit)

Customized for domestic and commercial HVAC systems, AHU heaters are used in air handling units as either a primary or secondary heating source. They are vital for managing proper internal and outside air ventilation, as well as air filtration.

High Density Cartridge Heaters

These compact, powerful heaters are designed for process industries. They are essential components in machinery for Food processing, Plastics, Packaging, Pharmaceuticals, Dies and molds, and specialized equipment like shoe and labeling machinery.

Thermocouples / RTD Sensors

RTDs (Resistance Temperature Detectors) are used for precise industrial temperature sensing from $-200^\circ\text{C}$ to $+2200^\circ\text{C}$. Thermocouples are commonly found in applications like hospital thermometers and in steel manufacturing to measure temperature for carbon content determination.

Process Immersion Heaters

These heaters are inserted directly into mediums for efficient Oil, Water, and Gas Heating. They are valued for their low maintenance, easy installation, cost-effective operation, and ability to sustain precise temperature control.

Bolt Heaters

Working via an induction coil inserted into the bolt, this heater provides rapid, precise heat to expand the bolt to a pre-decided length, making nut removal easy. They are typically used in specialized applications like Turbine maintenance in power generation plants.

Thermal Heating Process

Thermal Equipment Supply Process

We deliver custom, high-performance heating and temperature sensing solutions through a precise, end-to-end engineering pipeline. Our process starts with a deep dive into your application, analyzing the heat load, medium, and environmental constraints. This ensures the selection of the correct heating element (e.g., cartridge, immersion, tubular) and sensor (RTD, Thermocouple) for optimal efficiency and longevity. Every unit is subjected to rigorous quality control before final delivery and technical commissioning.

View Solutions

  1. 1. Requirement & Medium Analysis

    We determine the required heat load, physical constraints, operating medium (air, liquid, solid), and environmental safety needs (ATEX, IP ratings).

  2. 2. Engineering & Specification

    Selecting the optimal heater type, sheath material (Incoloy, Stainless Steel), wattage density, and integrated control sensors (RTD/Thermocouple).

  3. 3. Precision Manufacturing

    Element fabrication, bending, welding, and final assembly into flanges, screw plugs, or control panel housings, adhering to design blueprints.

  4. 4. Quality Control & Thermal Testing

    Rigorous testing including insulation resistance checks, thermal cycle testing, cold resistance verification, and calibration of sensors.

  5. 5. Delivery & Technical Support

    Secure, on-time delivery with complete documentation, followed by technical support for installation and commissioning.
